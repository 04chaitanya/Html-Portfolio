My top three movies of all time

Shutter Island

The movie is psychological thriller and keeps the interest of the viewer. Most of the viewers end up confused, unable to derive the conclusion.

Godfather

This is a movie series comprising of 3 movies and is based on a Gangster Don Vito Corleone and his family.

Top Gun

It comprises of two movies Top Gun and Top Gun Maverick, it is a story based on a fighter pilot in US Navy who faces difficult situations during service and always finds his way out rather than quitting.
